DecisionMaker Tutorials


These short video tutorials (2-6 minutes each) demonstrate how to apply DecisionMaker’s 10-step framework to a decision-making process. There is a brief introductory video plus one video for each of the ten steps in the framework. Click on each icon in the flowchart to access the relevant video. There is also a very short video that explains how to use the DecisionMaker flowchart. This series of tutorials follows an example decision about choosing digital math tools to support math instruction. You can download the full case study about this decision by clicking on the blue button labeled “Download Demo Examples” from your My Decisions page. The decision will appear in your decisions list and is titled: DEMO: Developing a Digital Toolkit for Math COPY.
