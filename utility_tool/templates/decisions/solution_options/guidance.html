Resources & Guidance

This page links to resources to guide you at various stages of the decision-making process. Click on the relevant step to view the resources available.

I Decision Problem Resources to help you identify the problem, define your goals, and define the decision problem
II Identify Stakeholders Resources to help you identify which stakeholders to include and how
III Solution Options Resources to help you identify possible programs, tools or strategies to address the decision problem you have articulated
IV Screening Criteria Resources to help you identify any absolute or non-negotiable requirements (e.g., compliance issues) that can be applied to narrow down the list of Solution Options to a number that will be feasible to evaluate fully
V Evaluation Criteria Resources to help identify factors you will consider in evaluating each of the possible Solution Options
VI Importance Scores What are Importance Scores and how should you assign them?
VII Evaluation Measures and Results Resources to help identify existing evidence or collect new evidence to evaluate how well each Solution Option meets your Evaluation Criteria
VIII Utility Results What is utility and how should you interpret it?
IX Costs Resources to help identify the resource requirements and estimate the costs of implementing each Solution Option
X Make a decision How to think about the results and apply them to decision-making
XI General Resources Miscellaneous other resources
